Research

I'm interested in 3D computer vision, motion generation, and diffusion models. My research focuses on generating realistic human gestures and motions from audio or text inputs, with an emphasis on personalization and controllability.

AdaptiveDiffuseMotion: Adaptive Multi-Task Diffusion Model for Speech-Driven Holistic Motion Generation
Enyun Xuan, You Li, Ziwei Li, Mengmeng Yao, Renzhong Guo
IEEE International Conference on Acoustics, Speech, and Signal Processing (ICASSP), 2026
Paper / Code

To address the challenge of coordinating facial expressions and body gestures, we propose a multi-task learning based multimodal generation framework that achieves joint modeling of holistic body motion driven by audio input.

Personalized 3D Co-Speech Gesture Generation via Style Extraction from a Single Video
Enyun Xuan You Li, Ziwei Li, Jiangqi Li, Renzhong Guo
IEEE Transactions on Multimedia (TMM) in progress, 2026
Paper / Code / Project Page

To address the challenge of quantifying personal speaking styles, we propose a novel paradigm that decouples style into kinematic and spatial dimensions, and design a new set of quantitative evaluation metrics. This enables personalized 3D co-speech gesture generation that captures individual speaking styles from a single reference video.

Text-to-Motion with Trajectory Control
Enyun Xuan
In Progress, 2025
Paper / Code / Demo

To address the issue that trajectory control in text-to-motion degrades motion quality, we adopt trajectory computation methods from Motion Matching to improve trajectory control performance while preserving the quality of generated motions.
